Who is Kabza De Small DJ?

From 2020 to 2021 Kabza has won many awards. In 2020 he won DStv Mzansi Viewers Awards 2020 under the category of favorite DJ, favorite rising star. In 2021 he got awards at SAMA 27 under the category of Male artist of the year, best Amapiano album, and duo/group of the year.

Kabza De Small DJ Career, Songs & Music Albums

In 2009 Kabza started working as DJ. He worked For a few years working as a DJ at various concerts and parties. Then in 2016, he came up with his debut album Avenue Sounds under House Afrika Records. Then in 2018, he made news by releasing back-to-back songs “Amabele Shaya” and “Umshove”. In 2019 his song “Bamba La” was presented to the audience in a collaboration with Leehleza.

His fan following and reputation increased when he collaborated with DJ Maphorisa and released the collaborative studio album “Scorpion kings.” Scorpion King remained hit as its 10000 copies were sold. Then again Kabza De Small launched his second collaborative album with DJ Maphorisa in October 2019 titled “Piano Hub” Last year in 2021 his album “Rumble in the Jungle” was released collaborating with DJ Maaphorisa and singer Tresor.
